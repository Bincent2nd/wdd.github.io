THE HISTORY OF TRENDYWEAR

Welcome to Trendy Wear, your number one source for all your fashion needs . We're dedicated to giving you the very best of all your fashion wear, with a focus to fits 3 main goals; affordability, reliability and quality. Founded in year 2018, by a small group of young entrepreneur, Trendy Wear has come a long way from its beginnings in a small city of Georgetown, Penang. .When the founders of the store, who called themselves, DCO3, first started out, Their aim for this store to reach out to local customers in support of product made in Malaysia.After their massive success and support from local patrons, Trendy Wear has started to expand their market to other states such as Melaka and Kuala Lumpur and now; it is one of the biggest fashion retailer store in Malaysia.Ever since then, Trendy Wear hasn’t stopped from expanding internationally to customers around the world. From the 2018 to the 2020, it was a very common fashion trend to see girls and guys of all ages wearing Trendy sweater,T-shirts ,hoodie .Girls of all ages and adults also wore the sweatshirts oversized or extra-long with leggings and slouch Today, Trendy is now considered a very well known brand in internationally.

Trendy has focused on the online shopping experience, creating a sense of digital inspiration by including visually rich large images with information on current fashion collections in the editorial section "The Agenda". The company has actively frequently uploaded posts of information on the patterns and styles, which highlight the craftsmanship of each product design. This has allowed consumers to gain a stronger emotional relationship with the brand.
